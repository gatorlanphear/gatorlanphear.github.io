How to Eat a Minature Sized Peanut Butter Cup
and hide the evidence!

By Gator Lanphear


Learning Objectives: By the end of this Job Aid, you will know:
  1. Ways to select a peanut butter cup
  2. How to open a peanut butter cup for consumption
  3. One way to eat a peanut butter cup
  4. What to do after consuming the peanut butter cup
Step 1: Get a bite sized peanut butter cup out of the bag

Open the bag of peanut butter cups and select one either randomly or choose one on purpose.

Usually, peanut butter cups are wrapped to look the same. Sometimes there will be an anomoly. It will not matter how or which one your choose for this procedure.

Step 2: Peel the outer wrapper

Carefully unwrap the wrapping foil.

This will expose the wondeful chocolate candy inside.

Step 3: Peel the fluted wrapper

Carefully unwrap the inner fluted crinkly paper wrapper.

By removing this wrapper, the peanut butter cup is exposed and ready to eat.

Step 4: Eat

Put the peanut butter cup in your mouth quicky, before the Dragons notice.

If you eat this while the family is away, they won't know you have them!

Step 5: Hide the Evidence

Clean up.

Hide the evidence before they find out!

Hide them in places where you know kids won't look! (behind books is a great hiding spot!)

warning Make sure these are sealed up. Do you want ants? Because that's how you get ants, Lana!
